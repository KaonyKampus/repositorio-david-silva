About me

Hi, I'm David Silva, a 24-year-old based in Bogotá, Colombia. I hold a bachelor's degree in Modern Languages. My passion for technology led me to pursue programming, and I am currently studying Software Engineering. I've completed several front-end programming courses for web development, and I am participating in the ORACLE NEXT education program, where I am training to become a back-end developer with Java.

My professional experience has been focused on customer service. Leveraging my communication skills in English, I have worked as a technical support agent for various companies in the BPO industry, including Asurion and Teleperformance.

I completed my bachelor's degree in Modern Languages in 2022 at Universidad ECCI in Bogotá. For my graduation project, I co-authored a monograph in English with a fellow student titled "Analysis of the Armies by Evelio Rosero: A View from the Subjectivity of Violence."

I am passionate about road cycling, which I practice regularly, and I also enjoy writing poetry.
